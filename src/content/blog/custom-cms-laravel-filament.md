Filament PHP has rapidly become the standard for building admin panels and custom CMS systems within the Laravel ecosystem. Built on top of the TALL stack (Tailwind, Alpine.js, Laravel, Livewire), it provides rapid development power.

## Why Filament?
Gone are the days of rolling your own authentication, data tables, and forms from scratch. Filament gives you robust field types, relationship managers, and sleek UI components out of the box.

Let's walk through how we set up our blog's entire backend in under 2 hours.
