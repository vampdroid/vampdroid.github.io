With constant Slack notifications, email pings, and meetings, finding actual time to code is harder than ever. Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task.

## Defending Your Time
Block out your calendar in chunks. If you have "Focus Time" booked, turn off all notifications and aggressively guard those hours. The context-switching tax on engineers is incredibly high. Let your team know when you're available and when you're strictly offline.
